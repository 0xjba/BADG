THE BADG PROGRAM

A milestone-based program to help you transform your web3 idea to a live project. Either create your own team or team up with some of the best minds in the web3 community. We’ll help you through various stages of building your solution.

MILESTONES

M 1.0: THE CONCEPT NOTE

JAN 01, 2023 TO JAN 10, 2023

Once you claim your Scribe 0 BADG NFT and successfully complete the first milestone and win the ‘Scribe 1.0’ badge, an NFT that you will always have in your wallet to commemorate this achievement and officially be part of the BADG program.

Tasks for this mission:

  • Develop your web3 hypothesis
  • Create a concept note
  • Summarise your solution
  • Submit the application

M 2.0: ASSEMBLE A TEAM

JAN 11, ‘23 to JAN 17, ‘23

Once your idea is selected, you are required to evaluate the skills needed to build your dream project. We will help you reach out to a wider community and bring in relevant, skilled members. Successfully complete the first milestone and win the ‘Scribe 2.0’ badge.

Tasks for this mission:

  • Identify skill gaps
  • Assemble a team or submit requests
  • Finalize & onboard members
  • Get to building

M 3.0: PROOF OF CONCEPT

SELF-PACED

Time to transform the idea to something meaningful. Leverage Arcana and its ecosystem partners to build out your use cases. Based on your product architecture, you might be eligible for grants, mentorship, and technical support. Successfully complete the first milestone and win the ‘Scribe 3.0’ badge

Tasks for this mission:

  • Build a working prototype
  • Provide a demo
  • Incorporate feedback
  • Create pitch deck/white paper

M 4.0: MINIMUM VIABLE PRODUCT

SELF-PACED

Once your POC is validated and accepted, it is time to build the real stuff. Build, test, audit and create a production ready product. We will help you with the right connects to raise funds and take it to market. Successfully complete the first milestone and win the ‘Scribe 4.0’ badge.

Tasks for this mission:

  • Build MVP in production
  • Get real user feedback
  • Create a product roadmap
  • Raise seed funds

M 5.0: PRODUCT LAUNCH

SELF-PACED

At this stage, you are all set to take your project to the market. We will provide advisory, co-marketing & other functional support. You are officially graduating from the BADG program & ready to conquer the web3 world. Successfully complete the last milestone & win the ‘Scribe 5.0’ badge.

Tasks for this mission:

  • Set up a company
  • Hire a full team
  • Generate revenue
  • Fundraise and more…

FANTASTIC BENEFITS

Besides providing guidance and technical support along the way, the BADG program will give you access to various grants, free platform credits, and wider visibility. You can also showcase your achievements with BADG NFTs that will automatically qualify you to various future initiatives in Arcana ecosystem.

GRANTS POOL

$100K

Win grants through various partner programs across multiple stages of your project.

GET FEATURED

You will get featured on Arcana’s Ecosystem page which provides you with good visibility and recognition. In addition, your project could be featured on the Wall of Fame on the website.

SCRIBE BADG NFTs

These rare NFTs - Not NFT’s that are provided to participants completing different milestones. Use them to open doors with investors or simply brag!

PLATFORM CREDITS

Get free platform credits that will help you build your POC and MVP.

GET STARTED!

Participating in the BADG program is simple. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Submit the application with your idea
Shortlisted teams will get a BADG NFT and access to private Discord channels
Participate in the onboarding sessions and get started
Complete milestones and progress to next levels. You will have BADG guide to help you along the way.
